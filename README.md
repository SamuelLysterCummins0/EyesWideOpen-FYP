# Lost Within 3DGameDev
 In a small town shadowed by dense forest, a man is put into a chilling search when his wife disappears under mysterious circumstances, last seen with a stranger. Driven by desperation, he heads to the forest, but a sinister, ghostly apparition of his wife causes him to crash his car, stranding him among the dark trees. As he searches for clues, he encounters eerie manifestations of her and stumbles upon her bloodstained belongings which causes the man’s other personality to take over and clean up the evidence, leading him to a secluded cabin. Inside, he faces a shocking revelation from a figure who looks just like him, his other personality, which confesses to having murdered his wife due to a deep resentment that had morphed into a violent alter ego. Overwhelmed and haunted by the truth, he runs out the cabin, only to be met by the police, who arrest him for the grim fate he unknowingly authored.      [Note: The theme “Roles Reversed” is seen in the game through progression and story, compared to the overall theme.]
